ICT for development

ICT convergence is driven by a continuum of advances in software, hardware, and telecommunications capabilities; exchanging synergies and creating 'endless' end user possibilities. Evolution towards converged service provisioning and consumption may take various alternate paths from current state-of-the-art in each of the constituent industry disciplines.
